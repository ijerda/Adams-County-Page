Adams County, Iowa Site Redesign

Over the course of several weeks, I worked on designing, refining, enhancing, and developing an information-heavy website for desktop and mobile. I redesigned the Adams County, Iowa website, focusing on making sure information was easy to find and understand for users. This is the final product.

All images below are expandable by clicking.

Before

The original side was extremely cluttered, unorganized, and bland. Much of the information on the homepage was irrelevant to users and much of the extremely important information users would go to the site to find was hidden within links and difficult-to-read text. Much of the information throughout the site was scattered in irrelevant places and difficult to find.

After

This project had me focus on making the information organized and visually pleasing. The purpose was to make important information easy to find, succint, and easy to read.

With the new design, I wanted it to feel like something you would experience in Iowa while remaining professional and easy to navigate, which is why I opted for shades of green and images of wheat fields and farm land.

Ultimately, the purpose of the design was to help users feel like they could navigate the oftentimes confusing government information they would need for every day tasks.
